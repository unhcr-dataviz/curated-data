Thematic data

UNHCR offers several global datasets on specific themes or sectors, which are important resources for regional and global analysis on refugees and other forcibly displaced and stateless people.

International Protection Monitoring Framework

RIMAP

The Rights Mapping and Analysis Platform (RiMAP) is an online Platform designed to provide structured data about laws and policies relating to the rights for refugees, asylum-seekers, internally displaced and stateless persons.

Situation Analysis and Needs Assessment

Refworld

Refworld is the leading global law and policy database relating to refugees, internally displaced and stateless people.

Learn more

Sectoral Operational & Solutions Support Framework

TIMS

The Technical Information Management Systems (TIMS) is a web-based tool for managing critical data related to technical components in displacement settings, including settlement planning, shelter, energy, environment, and WASH.

Learn more

(INTERNAL)

iRHIS

The Integrated Refugee Health Information System (iRHIS) is an interactive web-based application designed to collate and analyse a comprehensive set of health and nutrition data, providing insights into trends over time and generating various reports.

Learn more
LIMS, SENS, MSM, RMS

Frameworks for data collection and analysis in livelihoods, nutrition, multi-sector monitoring, and results monitoring.
