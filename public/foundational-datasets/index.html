Foundational datasets

UNHCR manages several important global data sets that are essential to analyzing issues related to refugees, displaced and stateless people. These foundational datasets are the building blocks on which all operational planning is based.

Refugee Data Finder

A comprehensive database with over 70 years of statistical data about forcibly displaced populations. It covers displaced populations such as refugees, asylum-seekers and internally displaced people, including their demographics. Stateless people are also included, most of whom have never been displaced.

GIS Database

UNHCR maintains publicly accessible geo-spatial datasets covering the location of UNHCR offices worldwide, as well as the locations of forcibly displaced and stateless people. These datasets can be used for creating maps and spatial analysis in any GIS software.

Learn more

*accessible through Orion

Nowcasting Database

Nowcasting predicts recent, past or present events to estimate numbers in situations where timely statistics are not available. In humanitarian and development situations, where collecting up-to-date statistics is challenging, nowcasting can be used to overcome reporting delays.

Learn more

*accessible through Orion (INTERNAL)

Refugee Funding Traker

The Refugee Funding Tracker helps partners involved in inter-agency refugee response to find information on funding for refugee programmes, including within the framework of Refugee Response Plans. The tool shows funding data for refugee response, starting in 2012.
