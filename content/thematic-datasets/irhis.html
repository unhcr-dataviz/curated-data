--- title: iRHIS weight: 30 tagline: featured_image: img/irhis.png link: https://his.unhcr.org/home intro: "The Integrated Refugee Health Information System (iRHIS) is an interactive web based application designed to collate and analyse a comprehensive set of health and nutrition data that visualizes trends over time and generates a range of reports. iRHIS provides country and global updates on standard indicators to inform programming and on comparative analysis with host communities across all subsections of public health." ---
