--- title: TIMS tagline: weight: 20 featured_image: https://placehold.co/600x400 link: https://intranet.unhcr.org/en/about/divisions/division-of-resilience-and-solutions--drs--/technical-support-section/tims.html other_info: "*(INTERNAL)" intro: "The Technical Information Management Systems (TIMS) is a web-based tool that allows users to manage critical data related to the technical components of a comprehensive response in displacement settings. Users can collect information on settlement planning, shelter, energy, environment, and WASH (water, sanitation, and hygiene) and analyse the data with user-friendly visualization and GIS tools. TIMS is a “one stop” data collection and monitoring system. TIMS helps field, country and regional offices leverage technical information to improve evidence-based strategic planning, reporting and advocacy." ---
